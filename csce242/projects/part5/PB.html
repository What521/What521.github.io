Puss in the boots

Long before meeting Shrek, Puss in Boots (Chris Miller) -- just named a hero for saving a woman from a charging bull -- is run out of town on suspicion of bank robbery, even though the real villain is Puss' friend, Humpty Dumpty (Joe Aguilar). Though there is still animosity between them, Puss and Humpty reunite to steal a goose that lays golden eggs. Joining them for the adventure of nine lifetimes is notorious cat burglar, Kitty Softpaws (Tom Wheeler).

Cast: Antonio Banderas, Salma Hayek

Year: 2011

Rating: 6.6/10 IMDb
